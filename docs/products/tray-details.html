Product Description

High-quality 3D printed organizer trays designed specifically for LEGO® minifigures heads and Akro mills small drawers. These trays provide a professional and organized way to keep your collection tidy and accessible.

Each tray is precision-printed with smooth surfaces and perfect fit for the Akro mills small drawers. Available in multiple colors to suit your display needs.

Features

  • Store up to 144 heads in each Akro Mills drawer (set)
  • 48 heads per tray
  • Interlocking trays to avoid movement when stacked
  • Durable PLA material
  • Smooth, professional finish
  • Compatible with Akro Mills small drawers
  • Printed Letters to help quick access to the minifigs heads
  • Stud holder to keep the heads secure

Specifications

Material: PLA plastic

Print Quality: 0.2mm layer height

Colors: Black & White, or custom (ask for availability)

Our own design: Anything can be changed in the model
